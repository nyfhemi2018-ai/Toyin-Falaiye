About Toyin Falaiye

Oluwatoyin Falaiye is a transformational leader whose work sits at the intersection of law, healing, advocacy, and purpose. A trained lawyer with years of experience in corporate law, gender justice, and human rights advocacy, she is deeply committed in corporate law, gender justice, and human rights advocacy. She is passionately committed to helping people heal, become whole, and live purposefully.

Her journey through abuse, trauma, depression, and suicide attempts became the foundation of a life devoted to healing others. Today, she serves as a voice of hope, restoration, and transformation to individuals navigating pain and transition.

  • Trauma-informed coaching & talk therapy
  • Gender justice & human rights advocacy
  • Leadership, mentorship & prophetic ministry
